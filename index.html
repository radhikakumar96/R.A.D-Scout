R.A.D Scout is a USC Roski School of Art and Design sponsored pop-up shop.

Put up by thirtheen Roski designers, it is a place to shop merchandise ranging from dip dyed t-shirts, lazer cut botanic badges, jute tote bags and much more!

Do come to join us to immerse in an organic experience. This is a change.
